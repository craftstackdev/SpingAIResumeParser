Order Status Explained
----------------------
ORDER CONFIRMED
- Payment received, preparing item
- Expected Timeline: 0-2 hours

PACKED
- Item packed and ready for pickup
- Expected Timeline: 2-24 hours

SHIPPED
- In transit to your location
- Expected Timeline: 1-5 days

OUT FOR DELIVERY
- Local delivery in progress
- Expected Timeline: Same day

DELIVERED
- Successfully delivered

NOTE: Tracking updates every 2-4 hours. If your order shows "Delayed", 
you'll receive automatic notifications with updated delivery dates.

RETURN & REFUND POLICY
================================================================================

Return Window
-------------
PRODUCT CATEGORY                    RETURN PERIOD    CONDITION REQUIRED
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Electronics (Laptops, Phones)       7 days           Unopened box, all accessories
Accessories (Chargers, Cases)       14 days          Original packaging
Smart Home Devices                  10 days          Factory reset, original box
Damaged/Defective Items             48 hours         As received, with photos


How to Initiate a Return
-------------------------
Step 1: Login to TechMart → My Orders → Select order → Click "Return"
Step 2: Select reason (Damaged, Wrong item, Not as described, Changed mind)
Step 3: Upload photos (mandatory for damage claims)
Step 4: Choose pickup slot (same day or next day)
Step 5: Courier pickup at your doorstep (free pickup)
Step 6: Refund processed within 3-5 business days after quality check


Non-Returnable Items
--------------------
- Software license keys (activated)
- Earphones and headphones (opened)
- Items without original invoice
- Products damaged by customer misuse

Delivery Partners
-----------------
We work with reliable logistics partners: BlueDart, Delhivery, FedEx, and Ekart.
You'll receive courier partner details and tracking number once order is shipped.


Delivery Attempts
-----------------
- First attempt: During selected time slot
- Second attempt: Next business day (automatic)
- Third attempt: You must reschedule or pickup from hub
- After 3 failed attempts: Order returned to seller, full refund processed


Unboxing at Delivery
--------------------
For orders above ₹10,000, you can request to open and inspect the package 
before accepting delivery. This helps verify the product condition immediately.
